  1. (8pt) Compute the average LE growth from 1960-2019 across the continents. Show the results in the order of growth. Explain what do you see.
averageLE <- gapminder %>%
  filter(!is.na(GDP_PC) & !is.na(lifeExpectancy) & !is.na(region) & !is.na(time)) %>%
